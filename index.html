FAQ

Common Questions

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले सवाल

Is this therapy? Are you a doctor?क्या आप therapist हैं? +
No, we are not therapists or doctors. Open Soul is a peer support and active listening service. We are trained listeners who offer a safe, judgment-free space. If you need clinical help, we will always guide you to appropriate resources. Hum dost hain, doctor nahi — lekin dil se sunne wale hain.
Do I have to turn my camera on?क्या camera on रखना ज़रूरी है? +
Absolutely not. Camera is 100% optional in every session. Many people prefer to keep it off — and that's completely fine. You can also use a nickname. Your comfort is the only thing that matters. Aap jaise comfortable ho, waisa karo.
Will my conversation be shared or recorded?क्या मेरी बातें record होती हैं? +
Never. Sessions are never recorded, stored, or shared with anyone. What you say stays between you and your listener. We treat your trust as the most sacred thing in this relationship. Aapki baat — sirf aapki.
How do I pay? Is it safe?Payment कैसे करें? +
We accept UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and bank transfer. Payment details are shared after booking confirmation on WhatsApp. No advance payment is taken before confirmation. Bilkul safe — koi fraud nahi.
What if I don't know what to say?अगर मुझे नहीं पता क्या बोलूँ? +
That's perfectly okay — and honestly, very common. You can start with "I don't know where to begin" and we'll gently guide the conversation. You don't need to prepare anything. Just show up as you are. "Pata nahi kya bolun" — yahi sahi jagah hai.
Can I cancel or reschedule?Session reschedule हो सकता है? +
Yes. If you need to reschedule, just let us know at least 2 hours in advance on WhatsApp and we'll find another time. Life happens — we completely understand. Koi tension nahi.
